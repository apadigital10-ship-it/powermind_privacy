Privacy Policy

PowerMind – Daily Affirmations

Last updated: 26 November 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the PowerMind – Daily Affirmations mobile application (“PowerMind”, “we”, “our” or “us”) handles your information.

PowerMind is a daily affirmations app available on the Google Play Store. The app is not targeted at children and is intended for general audiences aged 13+.

Information We Collect

PowerMind itself does not directly collect, store, or require any personally identifiable information from you within the app.

However, the app uses third-party services (such as advertising and analytics) that may collect information automatically for their own purposes, as described below.

Third-Party Services

PowerMind uses the following third-party services, which may collect information such as device identifiers, usage data, approximate location, and other technical details to provide ads, analytics, and app reliability:

1. Google AdMob (Ads)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob and its partners may collect and use certain data to show you personalized or non-personalized ads, in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

For more information, please see Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. Firebase (Analytics & App Services)

We use Google Firebase services (such as Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics or similar tools) to help improve app stability and understand basic usage patterns (for example, which screens are used most). Firebase may collect aggregated or anonymized data about app usage and technical information about your device.

For more information about Firebase, please see Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

PowerMind offers in-app purchases through the Google Play billing system. All payments and billing information are handled directly by Google Play; we do not receive or store your credit card or other payment details.

Any information collected in connection with purchases is governed by Google’s own policies: Google Play Terms of Service .

No Direct Data Collection by PowerMind

To clarify:

  • We do not create user accounts inside the app.
  • We do not ask you to enter your name, phone number, or address.
  • We do not directly collect personal information such as contacts, photos, messages, or precise location.

Any data that may be processed is handled by the third-party services mentioned above, under their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

PowerMind is not designed for children and is not marketed as a kids’ app. It is intended for general users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us through the app, please contact us using the email below so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

As PowerMind does not directly store your personal data on its own servers, there is no separate user database managed by us. However, the third-party services we use apply their own security measures to protect the data they handle.

Links to Other Sites

The app may display content or ads that link to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external services you visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date at the top will be revised. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed about how we handle information.
